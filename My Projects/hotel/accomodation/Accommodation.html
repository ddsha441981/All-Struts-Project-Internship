Accommodation

At Eastwoods Resort, we strive to make your stay an unforgettable experience. We offer 21 beautifully appointed guest rooms and suites scattered across our landscaped grounds, with spacious floor plans and cozy sit-outs. All our rooms boast of elegant and refined furnishing, modern in-room amenities and ultimate comfort. Whether you are planning a romantic escape, a weekend getaway or a peaceful family vacation, our comfortable rooms, unpretentious ambiance and impeccable resort services will meet all of your requirements.

To perfectly suit your needs we spoil you for choices, from our elegant Deluxe rooms to luxurious suites overlooking manicured, lush lawns. All our rooms are WiFi enabled and come with a mini bar, tea / coffee maker, LCD Television, hot water, air-conditioning and direct dialing facilities. We also offer dine-in options with a 24 x 7 room service, complimentary newspaper, laundry facilities and complimentary use of the swimming pool. Electronic safes can be arranged upon request.
